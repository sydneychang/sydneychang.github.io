UCLA's Suyama Project

The Suyama Project aims to preserve the history of Japanese American resistance during World War II, including the 100th/442nd Regimental Combat Team draftees, Army and draft resisters, No-Nos, renunciants, and other Nikkei dissidents of World War II.
